About these guidelines

Discuss #HearingAllyship on Twitter or Facebook.

This text was created with contributions from Deaf people engaged in the practice of social justice in an attempt to reduce audism, phonocentrism, and shift the balance of power and privilege within the Deaf community. This is a living document that intends to address the underlying principles of hearing allyship -- to that end, we welcome contributions and input based on what is currently relevant.
